#+Title: Spectrum
#+filetags: definition object

The spectrum of an operator $A$ on Banach space E, denoted $\sigma(A)$ or $Spec(A)$,
is the set of all $\lambda$ in $\mathbb{C}$ such that $(A - \lambda I) does not have a
bounded inverse.

For a [[id:7b68fd46-8ccb-450d-9708-21dc66ef66d4][Self-adjoint operator]], the spectrum is the union of the [[id:7273df75-67d0-4226-994f-8972b618f1e3][Point spectrum]] and the [[id:16f1b076-e7bf-40f3-8d72-14ad45449ef0][Essential Spectrum]].
