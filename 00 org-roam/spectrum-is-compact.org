#+TITLE: Spectra are Compact
#+FILETAGS: theorem

The [[id:b6c07176-104f-474f-a4d3-b34607ad64fd][Spectrum]] $\sigma(A)$ of an operator A on a Banach algebra is a
subset of the compact disk

$D = \{z \in \mathbb{C} : |z| \leq |A|\}$.
